04Why We Collect Data (Legal Basis)

  • Contract performance: To provide the Service you subscribed to — delivering daily screener reports to your email
  • Legal obligation: To maintain billing records required for tax and financial record-keeping under Indian law
  • Legitimate interest: To prevent fraud, maintain service security, and troubleshoot technical issues
  • Consent: For any optional communications, such as product updates or announcements

05Data Retention

  • Active subscribers: Account and payment data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus 3 years (for tax and audit purposes)
  • Cancelled subscribers: Core account data retained for 3 years post-cancellation; then securely deleted
  • Server logs: Retained for 90 days, then purged
  • Deletion requests: You may request deletion of your data at any time. See Section 9 for details

06Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following categories of third parties, strictly as necessary:

  • Razorpay: Payment processing. Razorpay receives your email, phone (if provided), and payment details to process transactions. Razorpay's privacy policy governs their use of your data.
  • Email delivery provider: Your email address is used to deliver daily reports. We use a transactional email service (e.g. Gmail / SMTP) for this purpose.
  • Legal authorities: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or government authority in India.

No data is transferred to entities outside India without adequate safeguards.

07Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Razorpay webhook signature verification to prevent payload tampering
  • Password-hashed credentials (we do not store plaintext passwords)
  • Access controls limiting personal data access to authorised personnel only

No method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you suspect a security breach affecting your account, contact us immediately at support@alphadominico.com.

08Cookies

The Service is primarily delivered via email and does not rely on browser cookies for its core functionality.
