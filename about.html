The story

ALPHAdominico started as a personal tool. I was spending too much time manually checking whether stocks met a specific set of technical and fundamental rules ‚Äî price above key moving averages, earnings growth, relative strength, and proximity to 52-week highs. I automated it.

The screener runs every morning before the NSE opens. It checks thousands of listed stocks against eight technical criteria and a fundamental overlay, then emails the results. The logic is fixed and mechanical. The same rules run every single day.

Over time, others asked for access. So I structured it as a subscription service. What you receive is exactly what I use personally ‚Äî a daily list of stocks that currently meet predefined criteria, nothing more.

I'm a solo developer, not a financial institution. I don't manage your money, pick stocks for you, or claim to know where markets are going. The screener tells you which stocks met the rules today. What you do with that information is entirely your call.
