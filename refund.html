Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 01 January 2025

This policy explains your rights and our procedures regarding subscription cancellation and refunds for ALPHAdominico. Please read it carefully before subscribing.

01Free Trial

7-Day Free Trial: New subscribers receive a 7-day free trial. No charge is made during the trial period. Your payment method is only charged on day 8 if you have not cancelled.

  • To avoid any charge, cancel your subscription before the end of day 7 of your trial
  • Cancellations can be done via the billing dashboard link in your welcome email or by emailing support@alphadominico.com
  • If you cancel before day 8, you will not be charged and your trial access will end immediately

02Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. There is no cancellation fee.

How to cancel:
1. Click the "Manage Billing" link in any report email footer, or
2. Email us at support@alphadominico.com with the subject "Cancel Subscription"

Upon cancellation:

  • Your subscription will not renew at the next billing date
  • You will retain access to the Service until the end of your current paid billing period
  • After the billing period ends, your account will be deactivated and reports will stop
  • No partial-month refunds are issued for the remaining days of an active paid period

03Refund Eligibility

ALPHAdominico subscriptions are for a digital service that is delivered immediately upon payment. As such, our refund policy is limited:

✓ May Be Eligible
First-time payment refund request within 48 hours of the charge — considered on a case-by-case basis at our sole discretion. Email us promptly at support@alphadominico.com.
✓ May Be Eligible
Technical failure where we were unable to deliver the service for 3 or more consecutive business days due to a fault on our end — full or partial refund at our discretion.
✗ Not Eligible
Requests made more than 48 hours after the charge, or for any renewal billing cycle after the first payment.
✗ Not Eligible
Refunds based on dissatisfaction with screener outputs, stock performance, or any investment outcome. The Service is a data tool — not a returns guarantee.
✗ Not Eligible
Partial-month refunds. We do not prorate unused days of an active subscription period.
✗ Not Eligible
Refunds where the user did not cancel before an auto-renewal that they were notified about in advance.

04Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Email support@alphadominico.com with the subject: "Refund Request"
  2. Include your registered email address and your Razorpay payment ID (found in the payment confirmation email)
  3. Briefly describe the reason for your refund request

We will acknowledge your request within 2 business days. If your refund is approved, it will be processed within 5–7 business days and credited to your original payment method. Processing timelines depend on your bank or payment provider.

Refunds are processed exclusively through the original payment method used at the time of purchase. We do not issue refunds via alternate methods, UPI handles, or bank transfers to different accounts.

05Auto-Renewal Charges

As stated in our Terms & Conditions, your subscription renews automatically each month. We send a reminder email before each renewal.

If you do not cancel before the renewal date, the renewal charge is non-refundable. We strongly recommend setting a personal reminder before your billing date if you wish to cancel.

If you believe you were charged erroneously (e.g., after a confirmed cancellation), contact us immediately at support@alphadominico.com with your payment ID and we will investigate.

06Payment Disputes & Chargebacks

We encourage you to contact us before initiating a chargeback or payment dispute with your bank or card issuer. Most issues can be resolved quickly through direct communication.

Initiating an unjustified chargeback may result in immediate suspension of your account and may affect your ability to subscribe in the future.

07Contact for Refund Queries

Email: support@alphadominico.com
Subject line: "Refund Request" or "Cancellation Query"
Response time: Within 2 business days
